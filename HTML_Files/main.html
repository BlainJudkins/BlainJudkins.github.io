Project: MindFocus

Team: The Segfaults

By: Ashley, Blain, Kylee, Joseph, Joshua

 

“Be the go to tool for everyday readers who want a more FOCUSed and customized reading experience.”

With resources such as the news and textbooks becoming more readily available online, it's becoming inevitable for users to read large chunks of text online. Sometimes, those chunks of text are difficult to read, especially if the font is small or the website lays everything out to be very cluttered. This difficulty leads to frustration and less efficient reading.

Our goal is to reach out to users and provide them a solution for these troubles. MindFocus is a user-friendly chrome extension that allows users to scale down their focus to smaller lines of text for more comprehensive reading by enabling a moveable “virtual bookmark” around the screen to cover up distracting text, as well as help them keep their place when reading on any website. The extension also allows users to choose from a variety of custom designs created by the team. Additionally, users are able to resize the bookmark to scale to their preferences.
